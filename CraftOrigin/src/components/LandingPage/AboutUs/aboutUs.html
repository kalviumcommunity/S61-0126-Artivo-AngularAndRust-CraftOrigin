About CraftOrigin

Empowering artisans, preserving traditions, and connecting the world with authentic Indian craftsmanship.

Our Mission

Bridging Cultures Through Art

CraftOrigin was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to create a fair-trade marketplace that directly connects tribal and rural artisans with art lovers worldwide. We believe that every handcrafted piece tells a story, and every purchase should empower the artist who created it.

Our platform ensures that 85% of every sale goes directly to the artisans, enabling them to sustain their traditional crafts while preserving India's rich cultural heritage for future generations.

Our Story

How It All Began

CraftOrigin started as a passion project to address a critical gap in the art marketplace. While traveling through rural India, our founders witnessed the incredible talent of local artisans who struggled to reach a global audience. Many were forced to sell their work through middlemen at unfair prices, barely making ends meet.

Determined to create change, we built CraftOrigin as a direct-to-artist platform that eliminates intermediaries. Today, we work with over 2,500 artisans across 15+ Indian states, helping them showcase their traditional crafts to the world while earning fair compensation for their skills.
